Systems that work as hard as you do.

Every business has processes that eat time, create errors, and limit growth. We find them and fix them — with automation built around how your business actually works.

Service

Workflow & Process Automation

If your team is copying data between spreadsheets, sending manual follow-up emails, or chasing approvals through chat messages, those workflows can be automated. We map your existing processes, identify the bottlenecks, and build systems that run them with minimal human intervention — so your people can focus on work that actually requires their judgment.

Example outcomes

  • Order processing that previously took 2 hours runs in minutes
  • Approval chains that complete automatically instead of stalling in inboxes
  • Status reports that generate themselves from live data

Service

ERP & Business System Design

Not every business needs SAP. But every growing business needs a system that connects its operations — sales, inventory, projects, finance — into a coherent whole. We design and build lightweight, custom ERP systems that fit your business instead of forcing your business to fit the software. Built to grow with you, not lock you in.

Example outcomes

  • A single source of truth across departments
  • Real-time visibility into costs, inventory, and project status
  • Elimination of duplicate data entry across tools

Service

Data Pipelines & Reporting Automation

If someone on your team spends hours each week pulling data from one system, reformatting it, and pasting it into another — that's a pipeline problem. We build automated data flows that move information between your tools reliably and on schedule, and reporting systems that surface the numbers you need without anyone having to assemble them manually.

Example outcomes

  • Weekly reports that build themselves from live data sources
  • Data flowing automatically between CRM, accounting, and project tools
  • Dashboards that update in real time instead of monthly spreadsheets
