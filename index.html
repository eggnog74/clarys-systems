Performing Arts College — Germany

A performing arts school in Germany was scheduling 300+ students by hand in Excel. Every term meant days of work, double-bookings, and missed classes. Off-the-shelf software didn't fit their curriculum.

We built a custom web app that generates schedules automatically, distributes them to students and teachers, tracks hours, and manages the entire curriculum database. It's still running today.

Government AV Integrator — United States

A major US-based AV integration company — contracted to install systems in highly secure military and government facilities — was producing project documentation by hand. Each project meant hours of cut-and-paste across dozens of Excel tabs for quotes, bills of materials, and cost summaries.

We learned D-Tools to produce proper system design drawings, then built Excel automation that populated every room tab, BOM, and cost summary from a single command. Hours per project collapsed to minutes, and the system is used on projects up to and including highly secure military installations.
